### 🎬 Project: **Animercial**

🧩 **Subject:**
3D interactive web portfolio and marketing showcase.

📄 **Description:**
Animercial is a visually rich 3D web experience designed for agencies and creative clients. It merges storytelling and motion design using Three.js to create interactive, cinematic marketing portfolios that leave a lasting impact.

🔧 **Features:**

* Real-time 3D animations
* Interactive portfolio navigation
* Embedded video and motion graphics
* Optimized for high performance
* Supports client branding customization

🧑‍💻 **Role:**
3D Frontend Developer – Designed and developed 3D visuals and animation sequences.

🧪 **Stack:**
Next.js · Three.js · React Three Fiber · GSAP

📆 **Duration:**
July – August 2025
